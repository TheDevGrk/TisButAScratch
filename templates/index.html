How to Play

Welcome to Tis But A Scratch, the combination of the classic game Hangman and a movie you might be familiar with: Monty Python and The Holy Grail. The objective of this game is to make it so that your opponent well... can't say hilarious quips like 'Tis But A Scratch' whilst they are losing their limbs! (I can never seem to find my limbs either) So now you may be asking: how exactly am I supposed to do that? By guessing the letters in your random 5 letter word of course! How else would you complete such a task? Isn't that how all sword fights go? Every time you guess a correct letter you get to... make your opponent lose a limb. (Jeez where do they keep going???) Be wary some of these words can be tricksters! Some words will have 2 of the same letter in them and only 1 will be revealed at a time! So if you are stumped, maybe try guessing a letter that you have already revealed in the word, you never know! We hope you enjoy!
